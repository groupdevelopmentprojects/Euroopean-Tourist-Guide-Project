The unusual shape of Las Médulas is a result of the mining techniques used here by the Ancient Romans to extract gold from the mountains.

Nicknamed "Little Switzerland", the Mulletheral region of Luxembourg is characterized bya picturesque hilly landscape and quaint villages.

Like something out of a fairy tale. Lake Bled harbours a small island, on which stand the Church of Assumption.

The Plitvice Lakes, an incredible network of some 16 lakes joined by jaw-dropping waterfalls, are one of nature's most incredible wonders.

Also known as Finnish Lakeland, this region of Finland is home to some 55,000 lakes.

You won't believe your eyes when you see the incredible formations at Brimham Rocks in Yorkshire, England.
